## What to include in your message

To ensure a fast and accurate response, please include:

- Type of document (article, thesis, grant proposal, etc.)
- Approximate word count
- Field or discipline
- Target journal or funding body (if applicable)
- Deadline (if any)

You may also attach a short excerpt (1–2 pages) for initial assessment and cost estimate.

---

## Turnaround time

I typically respond within **24–48 hours**.

Urgent requests are accepted according to availability and include a surcharge.

## Confidentiality

All documents, communications, and project materials are treated as strictly confidential.

Axiom Editing operates in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applies strict data protection principles to all personal and research-related data processed in the context of its services. This includes secure handling, minimal data retention, and non-disclosure of any content to third parties.

No document, excerpt, or information provided is shared, reused, or disclosed outside the scope of the requested work.

Where required by the client or institutional framework, a non-disclosure agreement (NDA) can be established prior to the commencement of any project.

## Workflow

### 1. Initial contact and document review
You send your document or excerpt with basic information about scope, deadline, and context.

### 2. Preliminary assessment
I review the material and evaluate:
- complexity of the text  
- estimated workload  
- type of editing required  

If necessary, I may ask clarifying questions before proceeding.

### 3. Quotation (devis)
You receive a detailed quotation including:
- estimated total price based on word count and service type  
- turnaround time  
- applicable urgency surcharge (if relevant)  

The quotation is non-binding at this stage.

### 4. Client approval
Work only begins after explicit confirmation of the quotation by email.

### 5. Editing process
The agreed service is carried out according to the selected level of editing or translation.

### 6. Delivery
You receive the edited document with optional notes or comments when relevant.

### 7. Optional revision discussion
Minor clarification questions or adjustments can be discussed after delivery if needed.
